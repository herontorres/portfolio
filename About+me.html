Get to know me more

I was born and raised in the vibrant northeast of Brazil, where I developed a deep connection with nature, especially the beach, which remains my favorite place to relax and recharge.

My seven years in the Air Force taught me discipline and core values that still guide me today. It was there I began honing my advanced Excel skills, using them to streamline processes and improve efficiency.

While pursuing my degree in Industrial Engineering, I juggled work and studies for five challenging years. This chapter of my life also brought me my greatest blessing — meeting Camila, my wife and Love of my Life.

In search of a better life, we moved to Toronto, Canada. The journey wasn’t easy; we faced many challenges working in diverse fields like construction and Uber. During this time, I began studying Business Administration, determined to rebuild our future.

Later, we settled in Greater Moncton on Canada’s east coast, drawn by its ocean charm and growth opportunities. Here, we’ve made a home, embracing our love for nature, fishing and enjoying the beauty of Shediac Bay.

Today, I work in the automotive industry, leveraging process automation to improve tasks across multiple departments. Committed to personal growth, I am now learning web development, expanding my skills to design and enhance even more impactful solutions.
